Maximum Power Transfer Theorem

  Procedure:

  1. Adjust the supply voltage to 20V.
  2. Vary the load resistance(RL) using the slider given in the circuit.
  3. Click on Simulate.
  4. Click on Add To Table.
  5. Repeat the steps 2,3 and 4.
  6. After taking your final reading Click on Plot Graph.
  7. The graph will be plotted.
